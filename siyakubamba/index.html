About Us

Why You Should Trust Us? Get Know About Us!

Siyakubamba is a black-owned company that provides medical case management, sourcing of supplies, mobility devices, and fund recovery services. Our onsite case managers provide initial assessments, recommendations, interventions, and rehabilitation services to help clients integrate back into their community.

We specialize in assisting road accident victims to maximize their benefits and improve their quality of life. Our target market includes injured road accident victims and individuals with special needs requiring rehabilitation programs and interventions.
